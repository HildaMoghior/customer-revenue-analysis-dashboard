# Customer Behaviour & Revenue Generation Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business intelligence analysis focused on two major business areas:

* **Customer Behaviour Analysis**
* **Revenue Generation Analysis**

Using Power BI, the project explores customer engagement patterns, purchasing behavior, sales performance, product performance, regional contributions, and channel effectiveness to uncover actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.

The dashboards were designed to provide a clean and interactive analytical experience while maintaining a modern and professional user interface using soft gradients, transparency effects, and consistent visual storytelling techniques.

---

#  Project Objectives

The goal of this project was to:

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior and engagement trends
* Identify the strongest-performing sales channels and regions
* Understand revenue drivers across products and demographics
* Detect weak-performing business areas requiring improvement
* Generate actionable recommendations to improve customer retention and revenue growth
* Build interactive and visually appealing Power BI dashboards suitable for executive-level reporting

#  Data Cleaning Process

The dataset initially contained several inconsistencies and quality issues that required cleaning before analysis.

### Cleaning steps performed:

* Removed missing and duplicate records
* Standardized inconsistent text values
* Corrected spelling inconsistencies
* Removed invalid age values
* Fixed inconsistent channel and region naming
* Recalculated revenue columns using:

```text id="formula"
Revenue = Units Sold × Unit Price
```

* Created analytical columns such as:

  * Month Name
  * Age Groups
  * Revenue Metrics

#  Insights

The analysis revealed that Instagram serves as the strongest-performing customer engagement and revenue-generation platform, indicating that social-commerce strategies significantly influence customer purchasing behavior. Customers aged 21–30 generated the highest spending levels, making young adults the business’s most valuable customer segment. Fashion products also emerged as the highest-performing product category, while regions such as Kano and Lagos contributed the largest share of revenue.

Despite these strengths, the analysis also uncovered several underperforming business areas. The website generated significantly lower customer engagement and revenue compared to social platforms, while some regions and older customer demographics contributed less to overall sales performance. This suggests that the business currently depends heavily on a limited number of successful channels, products, and customer groups.

The dashboards further showed that customer activity and revenue performance declined during later periods, indicating possible seasonal fluctuations, reduced engagement levels, or weaker promotional performance during those months.

Overall, the findings demonstrate that the business performs strongest where social engagement, customer convenience, and high-demand products intersect.

---

#  Recommendations

To sustain and improve long-term business growth, the company should continue strengthening high-performing areas such as Instagram, fashion products, and top-performing regions through consistent digital marketing campaigns, influencer collaborations, targeted advertisements, customer engagement strategies, and promotional content. Since these areas already drive strong customer conversion and revenue performance, increasing investment in them may significantly improve overall business profitability.

However, the business should also focus heavily on improving weaker-performing channels, regions, and customer demographics rather than relying solely on existing high-performing segments. The company should investigate why older age groups, weaker regions, and platforms such as the website contribute lower engagement and revenue by conducting customer surveys, collecting feedback, and analyzing regional purchasing behavior.

Improving website usability, simplifying checkout processes, enhancing mobile responsiveness, strengthening customer service, and introducing localized marketing campaigns may help improve customer acquisition and conversion across weaker-performing areas. Additionally, introducing more age-diverse product offerings and personalized promotions may help attract a broader customer base and create more balanced revenue generation across different customer segments and locations.

By simultaneously strengthening successful business areas while strategically improving weaker-performing segments, the business can achieve more sustainable growth, stronger customer retention, and a more diversified revenue structure.
